About me

Welcome to my page! My name is David Liedtka. I am a 2018 graduate of the United States Naval Academy, completing a dual-major in Computer Science and Information Technology, and an incoming graduate student at Stanford University as a Knight-Hennessy Scholar. I will pursue a Master of Science in Computer Science at Stanford.

I am interested in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and natural language processing. As a Trident Scholar while I was an undergraduate at the Naval Academy, I published work with my adviser, Professor Luke K. McDowell, on using links between different regions to predict elections as they unfolded in real-time.

I am a Cryptologic Warfare Officer in the United States Navy and will report to my first assignment after my time at Stanford.
